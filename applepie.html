Apple Pie


Make the perfect apple pie crust every time! This delicious dessert is sure to be a favorite.

Ingredients

  1. 1 package premade Pie Crust.
  2. 6 cups thinly sliced Peeled Apples
  3. 3/4 cups Sugar
  4. 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour
  5. 3/4 teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
  6. 1/4 teaspoon of Salt
  7. 1/8 teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
  8. 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Place pie crust in oven.
  3. Mix ingredients in seperate bowl and put into pie crust.
  4. Place bottom layer under top layer and seal edges together.
  5. Bake 40-45 minutes or until apples tender.
