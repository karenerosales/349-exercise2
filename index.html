Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup Butter
  2. 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
  3. 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  4. 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  5. 1 Large Egg
  6. 1 3/4 cups Flour
  7. 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
  8. 1/2 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  9. 1 cup of Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Microwave butter for about 40 seconds.
  3. Mix butter with the sugars until well-combined.
  4. Stir in vanilla and egg.
  5. Add flour, baking soda and salt and mix dough until soft.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 7-10 minutes or until set.
